History of La Nova

  • 1957

    A legacy beings at 43 South Niagara in Tonawanda, NY when Papa Joe Todaro, a carpenter and US Navy Veteran, opened La Nova Pizzeria with his twelve-year-old son, Joe Jr, and – as close as close can be – they created culinary greatness. That work ethic and motto: Customers Equal Family made La Nova a successful full service restaurant and banquet center.

  • 1971

    On January 6, 1971, the entrepreneurial Big Joe moves La Nova to the Delaware and Tacoma in North Buffalo. There the family introduces white pizza, steak and cheese subs and their now world famous Bar-B-Que wings to their evolving clientele. The business flourishes under the guidance and dedication of father and son.

  • 1973

    Big Joe moves La Nova to the family’s roots: West Ferry and Hampshire Street. Success with a great history continues.

  • 1994

    La Nova World Famous Bar-B-Que wings were made available to the pizza industry when Big Joe, his son Joe (LJ) and his daughter, Carla, started La Nova Wings. Today, La Nova Wings proprietary products are produced, processed and distributed in all 50 states.

  • 1998

    Papa Joe and family celebrating 40 years of business!

  • 2001

    The Todaros open La Nova Pizzeria in Amherst and it continues to thrive.

  • 2002

    Wing Stock - Chicken Wing Eating Contest, Buffalo, New York

  • 2011

    In a partnership, La Nova becomes “The official pizza of the Buffalo Bills”.

  • 2014

    In a similar partnership, La Nova becomes “The official pizza of the Buffalo Bisons Baseball Team”.

  • 2015

    The legacy continues at the Annual National Pizza Exposition in Las Vegas, Sorrento Cheese inducted the late Papa Joe and Big Joe as a founding members of its ‘Hall of Fame’.

  • 2016

    In yet another partnership, La Nova becomes “The Official Pizza and Wings of the Buffalo Sabres”.

    Todaro family was inducted into the National Buffalo Wing Festival Hall of Flame in Buffalo, New York.

  • 2019

    La Nova Wings celebrated it's 25th anniversary with Carrot Top hosting their annual chicken wing eating contest at the International Pizza Expo in Las Vegas.
